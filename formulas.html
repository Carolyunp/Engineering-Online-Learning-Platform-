Formulas:

  1. Spring Element Stiffness Matrix: \[ \begin{bmatrix} \bold{K} \end{bmatrix}^e = \begin{bmatrix} k & -k \\ -k & k \end{bmatrix} \]
  2. Beam Element Stiffness Matrix: \[ \begin{bmatrix} \bold{K} \end{bmatrix}^e = \frac{EI}{L^3} \begin{bmatrix} 12 & 6L & -12 & 6L \\ 6L & 4L^2 & -6L & 2L^2 \\ -12 & -6L & 12 & -6L \\ 6L & 2L^2 & -6L & 4L^2 \end{bmatrix} \]

Beam Element Shape Functions: \[ \bold{N^T}(\zeta) = \begin{bmatrix} 1 - \frac{3\zeta^2}{L^2} + \frac{2\zeta^3}{L^3} \\ \zeta - \frac{2\zeta^2}{L} + \frac{\zeta^3}{L^2} \\ \frac{3\zeta^2}{L^2} - \frac{2\zeta^3}{L^3} \\ -\frac{\zeta^2}{L} + \frac{\zeta^3}{L^2} \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & -3/L^2 & 2/L^3 \\ 0 & 1 & -2/L & 1/L^2 \\ 0 & 0 & 3/L^2 & -2/L^3 \\ 0 & 0 & -1/L & 1/L^2 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ \zeta \\ \zeta^2 \\ \zeta^3 \end{bmatrix} \]
  4. Work-equivalent end loads: \[ \bold{A}_{mE} = \int^L_0 p(\zeta) \bold{N^T}(\zeta)d\zeta \]
